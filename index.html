Brief History

DHP, Durhan Home of pets, is an international business organisation.It was started in 2000 Jan 31 by Dr John Drake.John Drake started it as a normal store, he used to keep pets products such as pets foods, medicenes and other products.
During this time, 2000 - 2003, very few people had pets in their homes.The benefits of having pets were only known by very few people.
Dr Drake decided to create awareness, starting from his own place to many parts of the country. In 2005 number of people having pets in their homes had increased.
This led to a need of more pets products like food, apparel, toys etc.Dr Drake with some help from friends expanded the only store they had. In 2006 profits from their selling wer enough to expand their business to other parts of the country.
DHP currentl has more than a million employees managed by a bord of 20 member, Dr drake being the chief executive of this cooperation.
